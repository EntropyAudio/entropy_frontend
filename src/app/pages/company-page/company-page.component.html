The Future of Audio Synthesis
Entropy Audio is a company focused on controllable AI and sound.
Our mission is to bring a new paradigm of tools to creators.
Human Creativity
We enable composers to create sound with natural language synthesis. This paradigm augments creativity for musicians instead of replacing it.
Controllability
Our state of the art machine learning systems understand music theory and sound design. Users comfortable with the technical side of music will feel right at home.
Democratisation
Natural language synthesis lowers the technical barriers and cost of obtaining high-quality sound, reducing the need for expensive sample packs or complex software.
